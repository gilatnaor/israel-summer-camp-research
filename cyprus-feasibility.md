# Cyprus Camp Feasibility Analysis

**Prepared: April 2026**

---

## Executive Summary

Cyprus presents a viable **premium add-on** to an Israel-based camp, not a replacement. Advantages: EU jurisdiction, safety perception (no rockets/shelters), resort infrastructure, 12.5% corporate tax. Downsides: regulatory complexity, parental anxiety about sending kids abroad, costs 1.5-2.5x Israel, kashrut logistics.

**Best strategy: Hybrid model** — run core sessions in Israel, add one premium Cyprus session in Year 2+.

## Regulatory Requirements (Cyprus)

- Register a **Cyprus Limited Company** (~EUR 1,500-3,000)
- Corporate tax: **12.5%** (vs Israel's 23%)
- Criminal background checks for all staff working with children
- Child protection compliance (2014 law)
- HACCP food hygiene, fire safety, pool safety (EN 15288)
- Work permits for non-EU staff (Israeli counselors) — **biggest operational headache**
  - Best path: recruit counselors who hold EU passports (many Israelis have Romanian/Polish/Portuguese)

---

## Target Market

- **Top 10-15% of Israeli families by income** (~150,000-200,000 families)
- Ages **12-16** (younger kids = too much parental anxiety)
- Optimal price: **NIS 8,000-10,000** for 14 days all-inclusive with flights
- Also potential for European families (Year 3+)

---

## The Hybrid Model (Recommended)

- **Israel:** Two 10-day sessions in July (NIS 3,500-5,000 each)
- **Cyprus:** One 14-day session in August (NIS 8,000-10,000)
- Israel = core product; Cyprus = premium upgrade/flagship
- De-risks Cyprus investment
- Creates natural upsell path
- Staff work both locations (solves permit timing)

---

## Key Risks

1. **Parental resistance** to sending children abroad (#1 risk)
2. **Medical emergency abroad** — medevac to Israel costs EUR 15,000-30,000
3. **Regulatory change** in Cyprus
4. **Reputational amplification** — any incident abroad gets bigger media coverage
5. **Scale challenges** — need 55+ campers to break even
6. **Kashrut logistics** — limited kosher supply in Cyprus
7. **Weather** — NOT the escape from heat parents imagine on the coast

---

## Recommendations

**DO:**
- Start hybrid model in Year 2 (not Year 1)
- Scout Cyprus Oct-Nov 2026
- Target Troodos-coast corridor
- Focus on ages 12-16
- Register Cyprus Ltd early (12.5% tax)
- Recruit EU-passport staff
- Pre-arrange hospital protocols

**DON'T:**
- Position as "safer Israel" (parents resent the implication)
- Try European market in Year 1
- Cut corners on kashrut
- Franchise before Year 3
- Assume Cyprus is cheaper (it's a premium product at premium price)

---

## Tax Advantages

- Cyprus corporate tax: 12.5% vs Israel 23%
- No withholding tax on dividends to non-residents
- IP Box regime: 80% exemption on IP profits
- Israel-Cyprus double tax treaty in effect
- **Warning:** Israeli tax authority aggressive about Israeli-controlled foreign companies — get professional cross-border tax advice (EUR 3,000-5,000)
